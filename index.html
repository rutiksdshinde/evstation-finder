Battery Care Tips

Common Questions

How accurate is the route planner?
Our planner uses real-time station data and your car's BMS profile. It's 95%+ accurate for Tata Nexon, MG ZS, Hyundai Kona. For best results, enter your current battery %.
Does traffic data update live?
Yes. Traffic layer refreshes every 60 seconds using TomTom Traffic API. Red roads = heavy congestion, yellow = moderate, green = free flow.
Can I reserve a charging slot?
Currently, only certain premium networks (e.g., Tata Power EZ Charge) support reservations. We show this with a "Reserve" badge on station cards.
